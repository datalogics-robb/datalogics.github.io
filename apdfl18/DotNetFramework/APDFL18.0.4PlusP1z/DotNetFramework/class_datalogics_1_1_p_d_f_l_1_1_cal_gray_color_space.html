Detailed Description

A calibrated gray color space.

A CalGray color space is a special case of a single-component CIE-based color space, known as a CIE-based A color space.

This type of space is the one-dimensional (and usually achromatic) analog of CIE-based ABC spaces.

See section 8.6.5.2 of the ISO 32000-1:2008, Document Management-Portable Document Format-Part 1: PDF 1.7, page 145.

blackPointAn array of three numbers specifying the tristimulus value, in the CIE 1931 XYZ space, of the diffuse black point. All three of these numbers must be non-negative.
